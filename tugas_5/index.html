Nobody knows. As far as I can tell, something happened last night. Something big. Our whole world was turned upside down. Town destroyed. Some weird, huge monsters comes out of nowhere. Some people survived, some people died, and others just disappears. No one knows why. Maybe you can help us find the answer.

Monsters?

Most of our towns were destroyed by monsters. They appears out of thin air, and just started a mayhem. Many brave warriors have died fighting them. We don't know anything about them other than that they're huge, bleeds orange fluid, and comes in different shape and weapons. Lucky for us, as a species who likes to kill each other, we have developed some weapons that can fight them. We call our weapon "Gears", a huge, mechanical robots we build to slay Gods. Maybe those monsters are Gods that we pissed by building these thing.
